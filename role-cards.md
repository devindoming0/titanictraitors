# TITANIC TRAITORS — Role Cards

Print and cut these cards. Fold them and seal in envelopes before distributing.

---

## HOW MANY TO PRINT

| Players | Print |
|---------|-------|
| 3–6     | 1 × TRAITOR card, rest FAITHFUL |
| 7–8     | 2 × TRAITOR cards, rest FAITHFUL |

---

## QUICK REFERENCE CARD

> Print one per player.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║    TITANIC TRAITORS — QUICK REF      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════╣
║  EACH DAY                            ║
║  Morning  — Murder announced         ║
║  Daytime  — Discuss & deduce         ║
║  Evening  — Vote to banish 1 player  ║
║  Night    — Traitor murders 1        ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════╣
║  FAITHFUL WIN                        ║
║  All Traitors are banished           ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════╣
║  TRAITOR WIN                         ║
║  Traitors ≥ Faithfuls remaining      ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════╣
║  VOTING                              ║
║  Nominate up to 3 · Needs a second   ║
║  Point simultaneously on count of 3  ║
║  Most votes = banished               ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════╣
║  MURDER SUBMISSION                   ║
║  Within 1 hour of dinner             ║
║  Privately to the host               ║
║  Announced next morning              ║
```
