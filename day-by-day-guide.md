# TITANIC TRAITORS — Day-by-Day Host Guide

A suggested schedule for running the game across a 6-day cruise.
Adapt freely to your actual itinerary.

---

## DAY 1 — EMBARKATION
*"Welcome Aboard"*

**Goal:** Get everyone into character. No murder. No vote. Just intrigue.

### What Happens
- [ ] Distribute character cards privately
- [ ] Distribute sealed role envelopes privately
- [ ] Run the **Traitor reveal ritual** before bed (see Rulebook — Setup)
- [ ] Players may (optionally) announce their character publicly at the first dinner
- [ ] No banishment vote tonight — it's a night of cocktails and suspicion

### Host Notes
Tonight is for immersion. Encourage players to introduce themselves by character name. If using the Suspicious Behavior optional rule, secretly distribute behavior cards to Traitors before or during dinner. Behaviors begin tomorrow.

**Opening toast suggestion:**
> *"To the finest ship ever built — and to the company of friends. May everyone survive the voyage."*

---

## DAY 2 — FIRST BLOOD
*"Something Is Wrong"*

**Goal:** First banishment. First murder. The game truly begins.

### Morning
- No murder to announce (Day 1 had no murder)
- Host may hint: *"The crossing has begun. Trust no one."*

### Daytime
- Players mingle, observe, whisper theories
- If using Suspicious Behavior: Traitors must perform their behavior at least once

### Evening Dinner — Banishment Vote #1
- Open accusations
- Nominate up to 3 players
- Vote simultaneously → 1 player banished
- Banished player opens their envelope

### Night
- Traitor submits murder target to host (within 1 hour of dinner ending)

---

## DAY 3 — THE MORNING AFTER
*"One of Us Is Lying"*

**Goal:** The first murder lands. Paranoia escalates.

### Morning
- Host announces: *"Last night, [Character Name] was found dead in their cabin."*
- Eliminated player is now a spectator (no votes, no hints until game end — unless Ghost Whisper rule is active)

### Daytime
- Discussion heats up. The first murder often reveals patterns.
- Were they vocal? Were they quiet? Why them?

### Evening Dinner — Banishment Vote #2
- Same format as Day 2
- Check win condition after banishment

### Night
- Traitor submits next murder target

---

## DAY 4 — MID-VOYAGE
*"The Ship Is Sinking"*

**Goal:** Stakes are highest. Most players have strong opinions now.

### Morning
- Announce murder #2
- Check win condition

### Daytime
- Alliances are visible. Traitor should be actively managing multiple suspicions.
- If there are two Traitors, this is the day they're most likely to be split by votes.

### Evening Dinner — Banishment Vote #3
- Consider: does the game end after this vote? Check table size.

### Night
- Traitor submits next murder target

---

## DAY 5 — THE ICEBERG
*"We're Running Out of Time"*

**Goal:** Near-endgame. Every vote matters enormously.

### Morning
- Announce murder #3
- Check win condition immediately — game may already be decided

### Daytime
- If the game continues: tension is at its peak. 3–5 players remain.
- Faithful players must be aggressive. A wrong vote here is fatal.

### Evening Dinner — Banishment Vote #4
- Final vote of the night voyage
- Check win condition immediately

### Night
- If the game continues: final murder submission

---

## DAY 6 — DISEMBARKATION
*"New York or the Bottom of the Atlantic"*

**Goal:** Resolution. Everyone learns the truth.

### Morning
- Announce final murder if applicable
- Check win condition

### If the Game Is Unresolved
- Hold a **Final Vote** at the last dinner (no murder follows)
- All living players vote to banish one final player
- If it's the Traitor: Faithfuls win
- If it's a Faithful: Traitors win

### The Grand Reveal
- All surviving players open their envelopes simultaneously
- Eliminated players reveal their roles
- The Traitor(s) share everything: who they killed, why, what they suspected

### Closing Toast
> *"My heart will go on."*

---

## QUICK WIN-CONDITION CHECKLIST

After every banishment and every murder announcement, check:

| Situation | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| All Traitors banished | **Faithfuls Win** |
| Traitors ≥ Faithfuls (living) | **Traitors Win** |
| Day 6, game unresolved | **Final Vote, no murder after** |

## SAMPLE TIMELINES

### 8-Player Game (2 Traitors)

| Event | Living T | Living F | Total |
|-------|----------|----------|-------|
| Start | 2 | 6 | 8 |
| After Vote 1 (banish Faithful) | 2 | 5 | 7 |
| After Murder 1 | 2 | 4 | 6 |
| After Vote 2 (banish Traitor) | 1 | 4 | 5 |
| After Murder 2 | 1 | 3 | 4 |
| After Vote 3 (banish Faithful) | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| After Murder 3 | 1 | 1 | 2 → **TRAITOR WINS** |

### 6-Player Game (1 Traitor)

| Event | Living T | Living F | Total |
|-------|----------|----------|-------|
| Start | 1 | 5 | 6 |
| After Vote 1 (banish Faithful) | 1 | 4 | 5 |
| After Murder 1 | 1 | 3 | 4 |
| After Vote 2 (banish Traitor) | 0 | 3 | 3 → **FAITHFULS WIN** |
